The Story Behind the StudioThe Story Behind the Studio

1994
Domestic & Global Expansion

Joined Gap Inc. to lead the domestic expansion strategy, architecting the enterprise design standards and prototypical frameworks that powered a rapid-scale rollout across the United States before pivoting to global market entry.

2008
Executive Oversight

Appointed VP of Corporate Creative Services. Directed $1B in global capital expenditure, spearheading new brand prototypes and flagship transformations in primary international hubs including New York, London, Paris, Milan, Hong Kong, Dubai and beyond – defining visual identity at enterprise scale.

2015
Hospitality Practice Launch

While driving retail innovation at Gap Inc., launched a specialized practice for elite hospitality, orchestrating dining environments for 3+ Michelin-starred venues where meticulous detail is engineered specifically for guest experience.

2020
Founded Left Coast Design Studio

Launched a strategy-led firm delivering brand turnarounds and market entry for retail and hospitality brands that value operational depth and authentic storytelling.

Today
Executive Oversight & Strategic Design

Advising global brands and leading the design of high-performance environments for elite hospitality and retail. I bridge the gap between corporate governance and creative execution, ensuring that meticulous design and capital discipline drive both the guest experience and shareholder value.
